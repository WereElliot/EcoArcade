EcoArcade

EcoArcade is a digital carbon awareness extension that estimates the emissions linked to your browsing activity, highlights the sites shaping your footprint, and turns lower-impact habits into a simple progress system.

It works by tracking active web sessions, estimating emissions from time spent on each site, and then presenting the results through insights, explainers, and rewards such as EcoPoints and EcoCoins for mindful actions.
